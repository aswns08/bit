제품관리

목록조회:
예1) http://localhost:8080/product/list
예2) http://localhost:8080/product/list?pageNo=페이지번호
예3) http://localhost:8080/product/list? pageNo=페이지번호&pageSize=페이지당출력개수

제품추가:
http://localhost:8080/product/add?name?name=제품명&qty=수량&mkno=제조사번호

제품조회:
http://localhost:8080/product/add?name?name=제품명&qty=수량&mkno=제조사번호

제품변경:
http://localhost:8080/product/update?no?name=제품번호&name=제품명qty=수량&mkno=제조사번호

제품삭제:
http://localhost:8080/product/delete?no=제품번호
